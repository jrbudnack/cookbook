# Beer Baked Beans
## Ingredients
* 3 Small Cans of Grandma Brown's Baked Beans  
* 1/2 Bottle of Homebrew
* 5 strips crispy bacon
* 5 strips half-done bacon
* 1 spoonful instant coffee (for smoky flavor)
* 2 spoonfuls dark-brown sugar
## Instructions 
Mix in a Crock Pot, bake in the oven at 350F (crock will take up to 400F) for 1 hour.Â  Don't try just cooking in the crock, as beans will not thicken.
