# Boiled Carrots

Source: https://www.allrecipes.com/recipe/18256/buttery-cooked-carrots/

## Ingredients
- 1 pound baby carrots
- 1/3 cup brown sugar
- 1/4 cup butter

## Instructions
- Cook carrots in a large pot of boiling water until tender, about 8 to 10 minutes.
- Strain carrots, leaving just enough of the cooking water to cover the bottom of the pan; set carrots aside.
- Stir in brown sugar and butter; simmer and stir until butter melts.
- Return carrots to the pot and toss to coat. Cover and let sit for a few minutes to allow flavors to mingle.
