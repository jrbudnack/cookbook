# Egg Muffins

INGREDIENTS:
6 eggs
1/4 cup milk
1/2 cup broccoli (or other vegetables like kale)
1/2 cup cheese
salt & pepper 

INSTRUCTIONS:
Preheat oven to 375 degrees
Whisk eggs & milk together
Whisk in cheese
Whisk in vegetable 
Add salt & pepper to taste
Spray muffin pan 
Bake in 375 degree oven for 20-25 minutes
