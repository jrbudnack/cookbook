# Smoked Pulled Pork

Source (Main Recipe): https://heygrillhey.com/simple-smoked-pulled-pork/
Source (Rub): https://heygrillhey.com/best-sweet-rub-grilled-pork-chicken/

## Ingredients
* 8-10 pound bone-in pork shoulder roast (or Boston butt)
* 2-3 Tablespoons yellow mustard
* 1/4 cup + 1 Tablespoon Signature Sweet Rub
  * 1/4 cup dark brown sugar
  * 1 Tablespoon coarse sea salt
  * 2 teaspoons black pepper
  * 2 teaspoons smoked paprika (hot or mild, whichever you prefer)
  * 2 teaspoons garlic powder
  * 2 teaspoons onion powder
  * 1 teaspoon ground mustard powder
  * 1/2 teaspoon cayenne pepper
