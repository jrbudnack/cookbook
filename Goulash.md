# Goulash

## Ingredients
- 1 lb box Elbow macaroni
- 1.5 lb ground beef
- Large jar spaghetti sauce
- 2 cans Campbell's tomato soup

## Instructions
- Brown the beef and drain, add back to pan
- Add ragu and soup and 2-3 cans full of water
- Cook pasta for 6 min in pot of boiling water
- Drain, pour pasta back into pot, dump all the rest of the stuff in.  Bring to boil, simmer for 20 min.

## Enhanced
- Use 2 cans of Amy’s Organic Chunky Tomato Bisque
- Use leftover can of tomato paste (left over from gumbo)
- Use 2 jars of Chef’s sauce instead of just 1
- Only 1 can of water
- Spices
  * 2 tsp garlic powder
  * 2 tsp onion powder
  * 4 tsp oregano
  * 1 tsp cayenne pepper
  * 2 tsp salt
  * 1/2 to 1 tsp black pepper
