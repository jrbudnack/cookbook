# Peanut Butter Oat Chocolate Chip Energy Balls

- 1 cup oats 
- 1/3 cup honey
- 1/2 cup creamy peanut butter
- 1/2 cup ground flaxseed
- 1/2 cup miniature chocolate chips
- 1 cup sweetened coconut flakes
- 1 1/2 tsp. vanilla extract
